This Poker Tournament Timer is a fully featured blind clock designed for home games and small tournaments.

Key Features:

  • Customizable blind structures with editable levels and breaks
  • Countdowns for dinner break, last rebuy, and total elapsed time
  • Multiple display themes (Dark, Light, High Contrast)
  • TV-optimized presets and fullscreen mode
  • Customizable text sizes, logo placement/size, and background
  • Custom sound upload for warnings and level changes
  • Draggable and persistent logo positioning
  • Keyboard shortcuts for full control
